Open since July, 2015. Empowering rural youth, children and vulnerable groups in Karamoja

  • About KAYEN
  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Values

KAYEN is the community based organization which was started in the year 2011 by a group of youth upon seeing how the region was suffering in terms of but not limited to marginalization, drought, illiteracy, domestic violence, human rights violation and HIV/AIDS discrimination aspects.

Therefore, Karamoja youth empowerment network was founded to respond and complement the work of other actors in development and government program to improve the livelihoods of people in Karamoja as a whole, and bring the preservation of culture and also bring down unrest from conflicts in Karamoja which is driving out peace, unity and the development of Karamoja out of the hands of the Karimojong, creating human rights violation and abuse in the region, and upgrading the suffering of the people of Karamoja.

To have a peaceful, healthy, empowered, self reliant and sustainable women and youth communities of Karamoja

Peaceful, empowered, healthy and prosperous women and youth communities of Karamoja

Scaling up the contribution of KAYEN performance in mobilization of people for protection, literacy, enterprise development, lobby and advocacy.
